Tirupati Starch & Chemicals Ltd. (TSCL) is a professionally managed “Wet Corn Process Industry” and is agro business based company having agro commodity operations from the last 35 years. The Company was formed in the year 1985 & commenced production of normal starch, pharma grade starch & Dextrine in 1989. Company expanded (Forward Integration) in year 1996 and started producing Dextrous Anhydrous and Dextrous Monohydrate.

The Company has received ISO 9001:2015 which certifies the quality standards of the products and processes which the Company adopts to manufacture its products.
